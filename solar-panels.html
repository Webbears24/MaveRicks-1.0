Solar Panels

Explore our curated range of high-performance solar panels — DCR certified for government subsidies and Non-DCR for private installations with higher wattage options.

ALMM / MNRE Approved 25–30 Year Warranty Top Indian Brands
DCR (Domestic Content Requirement) — Panels manufactured using Indian-made solar cells. Mandatory for government subsidies under PM Surya Ghar, PM-KUSUM & other MNRE schemes. Wattage range: 330W – 575W.
Non-DCR Panels — Manufactured using imported cells, offering higher wattage options. Ideal for private rooftop, commercial & industrial projects. Not eligible for government subsidies. Wattage range: 555W – 700W+.
