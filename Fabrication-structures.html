Fabrication & Mounting Structures

Heavy-duty solar mounting solutions — Hot-dip galvanised GI structures, roof railing systems, prefabricated modular frames and C-channel sections — engineered for Indian weather and wind loads.

Hot-Dip Galvanised Custom Fabrication Wind-Load Certified
Solar Mounting Structures — All structures are hot-dip galvanised (HDG) or pre-galvanised as per IS 4759 / IS 2629 with 85μm+ zinc coating for 25+ year outdoor life. Custom fabrication available for all roof types, tilt angles and wind zones.
Made in India

Built Tough. Designed for Indian Conditions.

MaverRicks fabricates and supplies solar mounting structures that withstand Maharashtra's monsoon, Vidarbha's heat and coastal wind loads. Every structure is engineered to IS 875 wind standards with structural calculation reports available for net metering applications.
