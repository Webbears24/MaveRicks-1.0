Complete Range

Inverters & Batteries

Complete range of solar inverters and energy storage batteries — On-Grid, Hybrid, Off-Grid & Commercial inverters with Lithium, Lead Acid and NiCd battery solutions from India's top brands.

4 Inverter Types 3 Battery Technologies Top Indian Brands
Solar Inverters — Convert DC power from panels to AC power for home/grid use. Available in On-Grid (grid-tied), Hybrid (with battery), Off-Grid (standalone) and Commercial (3-phase high capacity) configurations.
Solar Batteries — Store surplus solar energy for use at night or during power cuts. Lithium-Ion offers longest life & compact size; Lead Acid is budget-friendly; NiCd excels in extreme temperature applications.
Solar Inverters

The Brain of Your Solar System

Solar inverters convert DC electricity generated by solar panels into AC power for home and grid use. Choose the right type based on your need — grid backup, full battery storage, or large commercial plant.
